The Directions and Gameplay

This game is fairly easy. After pressing the play button the player is brought to the gameplay screen and the game starts. All the player has to do is:

  • Drag the player along the screen by pressing the keypad
  • Avoid spikes at all costs
  • Don't die
  • Have fun!

Not only that but you also have a score! The longer you last the higher your score gets, so avoid the spikes for a long time to get a good score! And with some fun music in the background this game is defintiely great for passing some time!

What happens if I lose?

You just start the game over again or you can go back to the homescreen that also has a place where you can click and play even more games!
